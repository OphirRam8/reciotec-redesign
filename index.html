§ 05 · Company

Israeli distributor. Long-horizon support.

RECIOTEC is a privately held Israeli company, operating since 1996. We stay involved from initial project definition through design, integration, and the years afterward — when support is what actually defines a supplier.

Selected partners, specified for the long run — and standing behind what's fielded.

RECIOTEC Ltd. was founded by Erez Ram, whose career in data acquisition and avionics instrumentation spans more than thirty years across Israeli and international markets. The company operates from Hod Hasharon as the exclusive Israeli distributor for three partners — UEI, Unigraf, and Great River Technology — representing them to the country's most demanding defense, aerospace, and space customers.

In practice: specification, selection, integration, and the unglamorous discipline of sustaining what was delivered — for program lifetimes, not quarters.
